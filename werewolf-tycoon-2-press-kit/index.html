Description

Become a Werewolf. Eat people. Dominate the world.

Become the Werewolf of Wall Street in this werewolf simulation game. Eat as many people as you can, but try not to be seen. After all, your plan for world domination could fail if too many people become aware of your existence!

Background

Werewolf Tycoon 2 is a collaboration between indie developers Joe Williamson and Sam Twidale.

The idea for Werewolf Tycoon started out as a sketch of two eyes peering out from a bush behind a park bench. Originally a “quick jam game” idea for their final year at university, it evolved into a Flash and mobile game that was an unexpected hit.

When fans demanded more, Joe and Sam started work on a bigger and better Werewolf Tycoon. In this upcoming sequel the Werewolf Tycoon sets sights beyond the local park and onto a more ambitious goal: eating the whole world.

Joe and Sam will live-stream the entire development of the game via Twitch.

Features

  • Be a werewolf and stealthily eat people.
  • Visit cities across the world and eat people.
  • Read about your global werewolf activity in newspapers.
  • Dominate the world.
  • Online leaderboards.
